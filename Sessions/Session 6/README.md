## 📖 Session Overview

Welcome to the **CV & LinkedIn** mastery session. In today's competitive job market, your resume and digital profile are your first impressions. 

A CV does not get you a job; it gets you an interview. This session explores the strict anatomy of a professional CV, how to beat ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems), and how to optimize your LinkedIn profile to ensure you stand out. Over 90% of recruiters use LinkedIn to find candidates, making your digital brand just as critical as your paper resume.

## 🚀 Learning Outcomes

### 🎯 Skills & Takeaways
- **Profile Architecture:** Build a strictly structured CV that includes a 2-4 sentence summary outlining *who* you are, *what* you can do, and *how* you add value.
- **ATS Compatibility:** Learn to format your CV cleanly and use targeted keywords so it gets shortlisted by software rather than rejected.
- **Digital Networking:** Optimize your LinkedIn banner, headline, and contact info to attract recruiters directly.
- **Content Strategy:** Develop a consistent LinkedIn posting cadence (e.g., 2 times a week on Tue/Wed/Thu) and actively engage with comments to boost algorithm reach.
- **Communication:** Craft a compelling 20-60 second "Elevator Speech" to pitch yourself effectively in any professional scenario.

<br>

## ✨ Session Highlights

> ### 💡 Important Insight
> *A CV is not meant to secure a job; its sole purpose is to stand out from other applicants and obtain an interview.*

> ### ⚡ Key Principle
> **The ATS Filter:** ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) scan, sort, and rank job applications automatically. If your CV lacks the right keywords or uses confusing formats, it will be instantly rejected before a human ever sees it.

> ### 🔥 Golden Rule
> **LinkedIn Rule of Consistency:** It is far better to post once a week, every week, than to post 5 times in one day and then disappear. Consistency builds your personal brand.
